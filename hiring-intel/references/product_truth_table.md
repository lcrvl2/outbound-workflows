## Core Features (WHAT WE ACTUALLY DO)

### Publishing
- Schedule & auto-publish to all supported platforms
- Post types: single image/video, Reels, Stories, Carousels, Pins, TikTok videos, X threads
- First comments & threads scheduling (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
- Publishing calendar (monthly/weekly views)
- Multi-step approval workflows with assignable roles
- Shared calendars (viewable by external stakeholders)
- Bulk publishing via CSV (up to 200 posts)
- Content queues (autopilot scheduling)
- Per-network text editing from one preview panel
- Media library with folders and labels
- Canva integration for in-app design
- Instagram product tagging
- Facebook post boosting from within platform
- AI writing assistant (enter copy, apply filters for enhancement)
- Hashtag management (saved hashtag groups)
- Best-time-to-publish AI recommendations

### Social Inbox
- Unified inbox: comments, mentions, DMs, reviews, ad comments across all platforms
- Includes paid/ad comments (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok)
- Label system for categorizing conversations
- Team assignment and routing
- Bulk actions (hide, delete, label, assign, review)
- Inbox Assistant: keyword-based automation (auto-review, hide, delete, assign, bookmark)
- Saved replies (Advanced+ plans only)
- AI-powered reply suggestions (adapts to your latest replies)
- Collision detection (see who's online/replying — Professional+ plans)

### Social Listening / Monitoring
- Keyword, brand mention, and hashtag tracking
- Monitors: social networks + thousands of news sites + millions of blogs
- Platforms: Facebook, Instagram, X, Reddit, news, blogs
- Sentiment analysis (tracks shifts, positive/negative patterns)
- Competitor monitoring (real-time performance tracking)
- Customizable crisis keyword alerts
- Real-time volume and engagement reports
- Up to 24 months historical data
- NOTE: Listening is a premium add-on (from $40/month)

### ROI & Attribution
- Tracks traffic, leads, and revenue from organic social posts
- Requires Google Analytics integration (GA admin must grant permissions)
- Attribution via UTM parameters (without proper UTM tagging, all traffic shows as organic)
- Tracks at post, conversation, profile, and team member level
- Custom UTM builder
- URL shorteners: pulse.ly (proprietary) + bit.ly
- PulseLink in Bio: mini-website with Instagram post-level impact measurement
- LIMITATION: Cannot distinguish paid vs organic without "social"/"paid" medium parameters in UTMs

### Reporting
- Content performance reports (reach, clicks, engagement rate)
- Community management reports (response times, message handling)
- Instagram Stories & Reels analytics
- YouTube & video metrics
- LinkedIn personal profile reports
- Competitor tracking reports (Facebook & Instagram)
- Power Reports: custom cross-profile reports with chosen metrics/date ranges (add-on)
- AI-powered report digest (summarizes key results in plain language)
- Export: PDF, CSV, PowerPoint
- Scheduled report delivery (weekly/monthly via email, Power Reports add-on)
- Labels/tags distribution in reports
- Optimal posting time recommendations

### AI Features
- AI writing assistant (content generation)
- AI-powered hashtag suggestions
- Alt text generator (one-click, keyword-rich)
- Reply suggestions with sentiment analysis
- Real-time audience sentiment tracking
- Best-time-to-publish recommendations
- AI report summary
- Listening wordcloud
- ChatGPT/Claude MCP integration
- All AI features are opt-in, human-reviewable, never autonomous

### Advocacy (Add-on)
- Employee/partner/influencer content amplification
- No app download required (advocates share via email link)
- Up to 5 copy versions per campaign
- Advocate segmentation via distribution lists
- Analytics: top advocates, channel performance
- Supported: Facebook, LinkedIn, X, Pinterest
- Free trial: 3 campaigns, 50 advocates max
- Full access requires paid add-on
